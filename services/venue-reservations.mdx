The **Venue Reservations** service manages bookings for special events, celebrations, and large gatherings at Picantería Lurwis. The system is handled by the **Agente Reservas Local** (Venue Reservations Agent), also known as "Wilson - Eventos".

<Warning>
**Implementation Status**: This service is partially implemented. The AI agent is configured and can hold conversations, but the database operations and human handoff workflow are not yet fully connected. The agent collects event details but cannot finalize bookings automatically.
</Warning>

## How It Works

When the [Clasificador agent](/workflows/procesador) detects venue reservation intent (mentions of events, large parties, or venue rental), it routes to the **Agente Reservas Local**.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Intent Classification">
    Messages are classified as RESERVAS_LOCAL when customers mention:
    - "Quiero alquilar el local"
    - "Evento corporativo"
    - "Cumpleaños con 30 personas"
    - "Reserva para boda"
  </Step>

  <Step title="Event Discovery">
    Wilson gathers essential event information:
    - Type of event (birthday, corporate, wedding, etc.)
    - Estimated guest count
    - Preferred date(s)
    - Special services needed (music, custom menu, decorations)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Venue Consultation">
    The agent explains:
    - Venue capacity and layout
    - Available amenities
    - Catering options
    - Pricing structure (if available)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Human Handoff">
    **⚠️ Pending Implementation**
    
    Planned tool: `add_human_service`
    - Escalates to human coordinator
    - Sends detailed event brief
    - Coordinator follows up with formal quote
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Agent Capabilities

**Wilson - Eventos** is designed as an event coordinator with the following objectives:

- **Event Type Identification**: Birthday, corporate, wedding, family reunion
- **Capacity Planning**: Gather estimated guest count
- **Service Customization**: Identify special requirements (music, menu, decorations)
- **Venue Presentation**: Highlight venue advantages and amenities
- **Handoff Preparation**: Collect all details for human coordinator follow-up

## Business Rules

### Guest Count Thresholds

- **2-12 guests**: Regular table reservation → [Table Reservations](/services/table-reservations)
- **13-99 guests**: Venue partial reservation (this service)
- **100+ guests**: Full venue rental with extended planning

<Note>
The agent should automatically detect guest count and recommend the appropriate service level. Large events (50+) may require deposits and longer planning timelines.
</Note>

### Lead Time Requirements

**⚠️ Not yet implemented**

Recommended policies:
- **Small events (13-30)**: Minimum 7 days notice
- **Medium events (31-60)**: Minimum 14 days notice  
- **Large events (61+)**: Minimum 30 days notice
- **Weddings**: Minimum 60 days notice
